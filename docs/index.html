Privacy Policy for Jobfluxor

This Privacy Policy describes how Jobfluxor ("we," "our," or "the Extension") collects, uses, and handles your personal information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

The Extension is designed to function entirely on your local device. It may collect personal information that you provide to it directly, such as:

  • Job application data: This includes information you enter into a form for automating job applications on LinkedIn, such as your name, contact information, work history, and resume information.
  • Interaction data: Information about how you interact with the Extension's user interface to facilitate its core functionality, such as button clicks and form field changes.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide the core functionality of the Extension. For example, your data is processed to automatically fill in forms for "easy apply" jobs on LinkedIn to streamline the application process. The information is used to improve the automation and provide a better user experience.

We do not transmit, store, or process any of your personal information on our servers or any third-party servers.

All data collected by the Extension remains on your local machine and is not accessible to us or anyone else.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data collected by the Extension is stored locally in your browser's **localStorage**. This data is not sent to us or any third parties. It is protected by the security of your local browser and device.

Because the data is stored locally, it is important to note that if you clear your browser's data, the Extension's data may also be permanently deleted.

4. Third-Party Services

The Extension does not use any third-party services that collect or process your personal information.

5. Your Choices

Since your data is stored locally, you have full control over it. You can delete the data at any time by clearing your browser's data or by uninstalling the Extension.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
